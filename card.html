Sloth

Sloths are arboreal animals, so they spend most of their time in trees
  • Scientific name : Folivora
  • Daily sleep : 15-18 hours
  • Higher classification : Pilosa
  • Average Lifespan : 10-16 years

Sloths are mammals classified in the families Megalonychidae (two-toed sloths) and Bradypodidae (three-toed sloths), including six extant species. They are named after the capital sin of sloth because they seem slow and lazy at first glance; however, their usual idleness is due to metabolic adaptations for conserving energy.Other notable traits of sloths include their strong body and their ability to host symbiotic algae on their furs.
